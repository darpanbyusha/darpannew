Our Traditional Process

01 Consultation

You visit our boutique and share your ideas, occasion, and preferences.
We listen closely and guide you toward styles that suit you best.

02Measurements

Precise measurements are taken to ensure the garment is tailored specifically to you.

03 Fabric and Design

You may bring your own fabric, or we can help source the right material.
Together we finalize the design, details, and delivery timeline.

04 Handcrafted Tailoring

Our master cutter carefully prepares the pattern to your measurements, and our skilled tailors stitch the garment with attention to every detail.

05 Fitting and Finishing

You return for a fitting.
Most pieces fit beautifully the first time, but we happily make any adjustments before the final delivery.

What else we offer
